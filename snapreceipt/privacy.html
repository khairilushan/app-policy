SnapReceipt Privacy Policy

Last updated: Saturday, 12 October 2024

At SnapReceipt, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our app. By using SnapReceipt, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

SnapReceipt does not collect any personal data or sensitive information from its users. All data, including receipt images, item details, and transaction information, is stored exclusively on your device. We do not access, store, or transmit any of your information to external servers or third parties.

2. Data Storage

All receipts and related information (such as item names, prices, categories, total transaction amount, and purchase date) that you capture or upload using SnapReceipt are securely stored locally on your device. SnapReceipt does not store your data on any cloud services or external servers.

3. How SnapReceipt Uses Your Data

SnapReceipt only uses the information stored on your device for the following purposes:

  • To display your receipts and their details within the app.
  • To analyze the items on your receipts and assign categories for easier tracking and organization.
  • To enable search functionality based on item names or categories.
  • To allow you to manage your stored receipts by deleting any data as needed.

SnapReceipt does not share, sell, or otherwise distribute your data to third parties. Your data remains private and under your control.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. SnapReceipt uses your device’s built-in security mechanisms to protect your stored data. However, it is your responsibility to ensure your device is adequately secured with passwords, biometrics, or other security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

Since your data is stored locally, SnapReceipt cannot recover any data if you lose access to your device or if the app is uninstalled. Please ensure you back up your device regularly to avoid data loss.

5. Third-Party Services

SnapReceipt does not use any third-party services or analytics tools that collect, store, or track user information.

6. Your Choices

You have full control over your receipt data within SnapReceipt. At any time, you can:

  • Delete individual receipts or all receipt data stored in the app.
  • Stop using SnapReceipt and uninstall the app, which will permanently delete all stored data from your device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. It is recommended that you review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about any changes.
